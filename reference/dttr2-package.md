# dttr2: Manipulate Date, POSIXct and hms Vectors

Manipulates date ('Date'), date time ('POSIXct') and time ('hms')
vectors. Date/times are considered discrete and are floored whenever
encountered. Times are wrapped and time zones are maintained unless
explicitly altered by the user.
